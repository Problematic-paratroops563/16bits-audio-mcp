### BGM
Best for level music, title screens, and calm background tracks.  
Use longer loops and a clear rhythm.

### SE
Best for short game sounds.  
Use quick tones and short bursts.

### Jingles
Best for win, lose, menu open, or item unlock moments.  
Keep them short so they do not get in the way of play.

## 🎛️ Good Settings to Try

### For BGM
- Use a longer duration
- Keep the tempo steady
- Use a loop if the app offers it
- Pick a sound that fits the scene

### For Sound Effects
- Use a short duration
- Keep the tone clear
- Avoid long fades
- Use higher pitch for light actions and lower pitch for heavy actions

### For Jingles
- Use a small number of notes
- Keep the sound short
- Make the ending clear
